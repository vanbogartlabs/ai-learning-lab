This document defines the investigative agenda for the project.

# Operating Rules

1. Every research artifact maps to at least one RQ.
2. Every source record references one or more RQs.
3. Every chapter brief lists the RQs it resolves.
4. Agents may propose new questions but may not delete approved questions.
5. Progress is measured by resolving high-value research questions—not by page count.

---

# Success Criteria

Before drafting begins:

- Critical questions substantially answered.
- Major contradictions documented.
- Evidence linked to each question.
- Remaining uncertainty explicitly identified.

The investigation drives the book. The questions—not the chapters—define project progress.
