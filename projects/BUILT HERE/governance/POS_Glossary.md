# POS Glossary

## Artifact
A durable record produced or evaluated by a responsibility.

## Responsibility
A permanent architectural contract.

## Agent
An implementation of a responsibility.

## Execution
Responsibilities that create artifacts.

## Governance
Responsibilities that protect system integrity.

## Perspective
Responsibilities that evaluate artifacts from a defined viewpoint.

## Authority
The explicitly defined permissions assigned to a responsibility.

## Debate
A structured challenge process producing a permanent Debate_Record.

## Approval
Authorization to advance workflow or accept content.

## Orchestrator
The governance responsibility coordinating execution.

## Preservation Rule
Previously approved content is never silently replaced; changes are explicit and auditable.
