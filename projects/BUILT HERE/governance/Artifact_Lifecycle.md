# Artifact Lifecycle

Every artifact follows a predictable lifecycle.

1. Proposed
2. In Progress
3. Under Review
4. In Debate (optional)
5. Approved
6. Published
7. Archived
8. Superseded (optional)

Rules:
- Every artifact has a single owner.
- Published artifacts are immutable.
- Revisions create new versions; they do not overwrite history.
- Every transition is logged.
