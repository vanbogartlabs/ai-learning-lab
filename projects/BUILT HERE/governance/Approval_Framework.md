# Approval Framework

## Purpose
Defines when work proceeds autonomously and when the Human Author must approve.

## Approval Levels

### A0 – Autonomous
Routine execution. No human interaction required.

Examples:
- Formatting
- Citation generation
- OCR cleanup
- Repository housekeeping

### A1 – Notify
Continue automatically but record the decision.

Examples:
- Research completion
- Draft completion
- QA pass

### A2 – Human Review Required
Pause until author reviews.

Examples:
- Chapter brief
- First chapter draft
- Final chapter draft
- Book structure changes

### A3 – Constitutional Ratification
Only the Human Author may approve.

Examples:
- Constitutional amendments
- New responsibility classes
- Changes to First Principles

## Principle

> Automation is the default. Human approval is reserved for decisions that change meaning, direction, or governance.
