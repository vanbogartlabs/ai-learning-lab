# CHAPTER CONTRACT

Every chapter must contain:

1. One entrepreneur as the clear protagonist.
2. A meaningful problem introduced immediately.
3. A business journey driven by decisions.
4. Cedar Rapids revealed through the entrepreneur.
5. Real stakes.
6. One pivotal decision.
7. A satisfying outcome.
8. A lasting legacy.
9. A bridge into the next chapter.
10. Emotional movement.
11. Story, not chronology.
12. A one-sentence takeaway someone would repeat to a friend.

Final question:

**Would someone who has never heard of Cedar Rapids keep reading because they care about this entrepreneur?**
