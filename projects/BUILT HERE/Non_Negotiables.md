# BUILT HERE — NON-NEGOTIABLES

These rules apply to every agent.

## 1. Never bore the reader.

## 2. Every chapter tells one unforgettable entrepreneurial story.

## 3. Entrepreneurs always come before history.

## 4. Every page earns the next page.

## 5. Historical integrity is mandatory. Academic timidity is not.

## 6. Every chapter contains:
- conflict
- risk
- decision
- consequence
- legacy

## 7. The city is revealed through its builders.

## 8. Every chapter strengthens the central thesis:
Generations of entrepreneurs helped build an extraordinary town, and that town developed a culture that continued producing extraordinary entrepreneurs.

## 9. The ending of the book must emotionally fulfill the promise made in the opening.

## 10. The reader closes the book inspired to build something.
