# BUILT HERE — COURAGE PRINCIPLES

## Purpose

Built Here is not a catalog of successful businesses.

It is an examination of courageous decisions made under uncertainty.

Every entrepreneur in this book faced a moment when waiting was safer, consensus was against them, the outcome was unknowable, and action required conviction.

The purpose of this system is to reveal those moments faithfully.

---

# First Principle

**Courage is the engine of the story.**

Facts establish what happened.

Courage explains why the story matters.

Whenever the writing drifts toward chronology, institutions, or civic history, return to the courageous decision at the center of the chapter.

---

# Definition of Entrepreneurial Courage

Entrepreneurial courage is:

> Choosing meaningful action under uncertainty while accepting personal responsibility for the outcome.

It is not recklessness.

It is not optimism.

It is not confidence after success.

It is disciplined commitment before success is visible.

---

# Every Chapter Must Answer

Every chapter must answer these questions:

1. What future did this entrepreneur believe in before the evidence existed?
2. What meaningful risks did they knowingly accept?
3. What would have happened if they had chosen to wait?
4. Why did reasonable people doubt them?
5. What courageous decision changed the trajectory of the story?
6. What inheritance did that decision leave for the next builder?

If a chapter cannot answer these questions, it is not finished.

---

# The Courage Test

Every entrepreneur should make the reader think:

"I'm not sure I would have had the courage to do that."

If the reader only learns facts, the chapter has failed.

If the reader understands the weight of the decision, the chapter has succeeded.

---

# Rules for Every Agent

Every agent must optimize for revealing courage without sacrificing historical integrity.

Research Agent:
Find evidence of uncertainty, opposition, tradeoffs, and consequences—not just dates and events.

Character Brief Agent:
Identify what the entrepreneur believed, feared, protected, and was willing to risk.

Chapter Intent Agent:
Define the single courageous decision that drives the chapter.

Writer Agent:
Place the reader beside the entrepreneur before, during, and after the defining decision.

Narrative Enrichment Agent:
Strengthen the emotional weight of decisions without inventing facts or motives.

Fact Checker:
Protect truth while preserving the force of the narrative.

---

# Historical Integrity

Never manufacture courage.

Never invent dialogue.

Never invent emotions.

Never invent motives.

Instead, assemble the documented evidence so readers naturally experience the courage already present in the historical record.

Truth is never the enemy of compelling storytelling.

---

# The Standard

A successful chapter allows the reader to answer:

"What impossible thing did this entrepreneur choose to attempt?"

A successful book allows the reader to answer:

"What kind of culture is created when generations of people repeatedly make courageous decisions?"

That is the question Built Here exists to explore.
