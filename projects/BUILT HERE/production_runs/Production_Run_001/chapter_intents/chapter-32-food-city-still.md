## Central Business Problem

What does Cedar Rapids still possess when its corn-processing system remains useful across multiple products but a global corporation chooses how intensely it operates, what uses it emphasizes, and which workers receive the immediate uncertainty?

The conflict is corporate option versus local constraint. Physical flexibility creates choices at portfolio scale, but neither workers nor the city hold demonstrated final local control over those choices.
