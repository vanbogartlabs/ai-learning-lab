In March 2022 Almost Famous acquired Noble Popcorn in Sac City, expanding operations beyond Cedar Rapids and Des Moines. The 2022 acquisition expanded operating footprint and scale. The inspected sources do not disclose purchase price, debt, equity, guarantees, retained obligations, staffing commitments, losses, or setbacks.

### Safer alternatives

For the family in 2014: remain at NewBo, reduce hours, keep the produce/popcorn/ice-cream activities separate, delay leasing, or stop one line. For the company in 2022: grow organically, contract production, decline the acquisition, or expand only in Cedar Rapids. These are analytical alternatives; no source attributes an evaluated option set to Sydney.
