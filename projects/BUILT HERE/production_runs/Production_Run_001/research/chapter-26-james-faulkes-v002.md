The narrative spine must allocate roles instead of collapsing them. Faulkes and Thompson may appear only as the site's apparent co-owners or associated owners. Public bodies and infrastructure shaped access without becoming proof of Faulkes's private authority.

The interval between announcement, construction, and opening can establish project uncertainty. It cannot be called Faulkes's capital wait or wager. The project-level consequence is supportable: an outlying retail center opened with thirty stores and three anchors. Faulkes's financial or personal consequence is unknown.
