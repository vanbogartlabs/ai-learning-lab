# Narrative Discovery Record — Chapter 7: Anna Jacobs and Anna Kurik

## The One Truth

The strongest record will not support an Anna Jacobs solo story: Jacobs and Anna Kurik jointly operated Jacobs & Kurik Drygoods and were collectively credited with leasing space for a time to Anna Lesinger, an undertaker. Three named women appear in distinct commercial roles, but neither co-operator may be elevated above the other.

## Why It Matters

The joint firm name is the unforgettable reason. It prevents a building called “Jacobs” from erasing Kurik and turns anonymous commerce into named, shared activity. The remarkable truth is not individual bravery; the evidence preserves co-agency and another woman’s separate enterprise while withholding ownership, authority, motive, risk, and result.

## Entrepreneurial Meaning
