# Narrative Discovery Record — Chapter 29: Gail Naughton

## The One Unforgettable Truth

While NCSML remained inaccessible and its losses could not be counted, Gail Naughton publicly committed the institution to continue preserving Czech and Slovak heritage and to rebuild; the removal of two semi-truck loads and emergency planning remained collective board-and-staff work, not her proved authorization.

## Why It Is Unforgettable

The reversal is the chapter: Naughton could make the mission publicly answerable before anyone could make the building knowable. Her named commitment mattered precisely because it did not confer command. This is distinct from Chapter 30’s later conservation, financing, relocation, elevation, expansion, and construction program.
