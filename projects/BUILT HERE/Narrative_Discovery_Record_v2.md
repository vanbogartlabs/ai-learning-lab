# Narrative Discovery Record (NDR)

## Purpose

The Narrative Discovery Record is **not** a research report.

It is the editorial contract between research and writing.

Its purpose is to preserve the one historically supported truth that must survive every draft, revision, and edit.

A writer should be able to read this document in under two minutes.

---

# Entrepreneur

---

# The One Truth

State the single historically supported truth that most fundamentally changes how readers understand this entrepreneur.

Limit: 75 words.

---

# Why It Matters

Explain why this truth matters.

Answer:

- Why is it memorable?
- Why does it change how we understand the entrepreneur?
- Why would another entrepreneur care?

Limit: 150 words.

---

# Courage Revealed

Complete:

> Despite ____________________, the entrepreneur chose to ____________________ because they believed ____________________.

This sentence should become the emotional center of the chapter.

---

# Reader-Retell Sentence

If a reader remembers only one thing ten years from now, it should be:

> _______________________________

---

# Placement Requirements

Required appearance:

- Opening line
- Opening page
- First 10%
- Later reveal (justify)

Explain why.

---

# Evidence Boundary

## Supported Facts

List only the minimum facts required to support the One Truth.

## Required Qualifications

List only qualifications that materially change meaning.

Do not reproduce research notes.

# What Must Never Be Added

Examples:

- unsupported motives
- invented dialogue
- exaggerated risk
- founder worship
- unsupported causal claims

Add entrepreneur-specific cautions as needed.

---

# Relationship to Other Artifacts

Character Brief answers:

> Who was this entrepreneur?

Narrative Discovery Record answers:

> Why will readers never forget this entrepreneur?

Chapter Intent answers:

> What story are we telling?

These artifacts must complement one another rather than duplicate content.

---

# Approval Test

Approve only if:

- one unforgettable truth has been selected
- the courageous decision is unmistakable
- evidence supports every major claim
- the writer can understand the chapter's narrative priority in under two minutes
- removing the One Truth would materially weaken the chapter

If any answer is no, the Narrative Discovery Record is incomplete.
